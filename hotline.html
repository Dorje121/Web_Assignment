Emergency Hotlines

If you or someone you know is in need of urgent help, please reach out to the following hotlines. These services are available 24/7 and provide confidential support to anyone in crisis or facing difficult situations.

  • Mental Hospital- Rehabilitation Center: 1-800-273-TALK (8255)
  • Rhythm Neuropsychiatry Hospital and Research Center Text HOME to 741741
  • Mental Treatment Rehabilitation Center 1-800-799-SAFE (7233)
  • Chaitanya Institute for Mental Health 1-800-662-HELP (4357)
